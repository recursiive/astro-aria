## Room Overview

The peaceful town of Snowpine is getting ready for their biggest Winter Festival celebration. But a dark force called "The Frost Shadow" has come from the winter mist and wants to ruin the festival and steal all the joy from families. The town's wise elders left behind five special guardian skills to protect against such threats. You are chosen to learn these skills and become the new protector. Master all five training challenges to prove you can save the Winter Festival and keep everyone safe.
